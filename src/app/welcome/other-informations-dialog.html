Dashboard page

Click on the dashboard page to explore the following:

  • Pedestrian count, routing algorithm, and other valuable information.
  • Real-time data collected from sensors in smart bins located in Argyle Square (Melbourne, Australia).
  • Various sensors, including bins, weather, and pedestrian count sensors, continuously updated every few seconds.
  • Each of the 24 smart bins provides data on temperature, fill level, battery status, and geographic coordinates (latitude & longitude).

Alarms page

The Alarms section provides insights into the bin's current status:

  • Monitor bins' fill levels, temperature, and battery life.
